Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about our services. If you have any other questions, feel free to contact us.

  • What services do you offer?

    We offer a range of services including web development, resume website creation, and website maintenance. We also provide customized solutions based on your specific needs.

  • What is included in the setup fee?

    The setup fee includes the initial consultation, customized design and layout of your website, and the integration of any necessary features or functionalities.

  • How does the billing work?

    For our web services, there is a one-time setup fee plus a monthly charge. For resume websites, there is a flat monthly fee. All fees are billed monthly and can be cancelled at any time.

  • What if I have specific needs that aren't listed in your services?

    We can provide customized solutions to meet any specific needs you may have. Please contact us to discuss your requirements and we will provide a quote based on the complexity and scope of the work.

  • What is the process for getting started?

    To get started, simply contact us and we will schedule an initial consultation. During this consultation, we will discuss your needs, goals, and preferences. We will then create a proposal for your review.
